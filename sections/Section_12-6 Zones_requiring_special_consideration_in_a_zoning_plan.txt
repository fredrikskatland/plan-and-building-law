Section 12-6.Â Zones requiring special consideration in a zoning plan
The considerations and restrictions stipulated with respect to the land-use element of the municipal master plan by way of zones requiring special consideration, see sections 11-8 and 11-10, shall be used as the basis for preparation of a zoning plan. Zones requiring special consideration may be continued in the zoning plan or be incorporated in land-use objectives and provisions that safeguard the purpose of the zoning plan.
