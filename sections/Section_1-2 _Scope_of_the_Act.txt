Section 1-2.Â  Scope of the Act
Unless otherwise provided, the Act applies to the entire country, including river systems.
In marine areas, the Act applies to a zone extending one nautical mile beyond the baselines of the territorial sea.
The King may decide that Chapter 14 shall apply to specific projects outside one nautical mile beyond the baselines.
The King may decide that the Act shall apply in whole or in part to Svalbard.
