Section 8-1.Â  Regional master plans
The regional planning authority shall prepare regional master plans that address the issues identified in the regional planning strategy.
The King may make orders regarding the preparation of regional master plans for specific areas of activity, topics or geographical areas and through regulations lay down further provisions regarding content, organisation and whether the plan shall be approved by the King.
A programme of action shall be drawn up for implementing the regional master plan.
The programme of action shall be adopted by the regional planning authority and shall be rolled over annually.
