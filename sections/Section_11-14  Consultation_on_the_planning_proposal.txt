Section 11-14.  Consultation on the planning proposal
The proposal for the social element of the municipal master plan and the land-use element of the municipal master plan shall be circulated for comment and presented for public scrutiny, announced in at least one newspaper that is commonly read in the locality and be made available through electronic media. The time limit for comment and for making any objection to the land-use element of the municipal master plan shall be at least six weeks.
The case presentation shall show how the effects of the plan and comments received on the planning proposal have been assessed, and what significance they are assigned in the decision adopted. The presentation shall also show how the plan takes national and regional interests into consideration.
