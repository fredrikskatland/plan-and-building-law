Section 1-5. Effects of plans
The effect of plans adopted pursuant to this Act on further planning, management and administrative decisions regarding activities, physical development and conservation is evident from the provisions for the various types of plan, see sections 6-3, 8-2, 8-5, 11-3, 11-6 and 12-4.
In the event of conflict, a new plan or central government or regional planning provision prevails over an older plan or planning provision for the same land unless otherwise determined in the new plan or central government or regional planning provision.
